What is Creative Fatigue?

Creative Fatigue = When your audience has seen your ad so many times, they ignore it.

Result: CTR drops, CPA rises, ROAS tanks. It's the #1 killer of profitable campaigns.

This course teaches you how to detect fatigue BEFORE it kills your ROAS.

🚨 The 5 Warning Signs

Signal #1: Frequency > 4.0

Average person has seen your ad 4+ times. They're tired of it.

Action: Pause ad immediately. Launch new creative.

Signal #2: CTR drops > 30% week-over-week

People are ignoring your ad. Hook is stale.

Action: Test new hook variations.

Signal #3: CPM increasing (but targeting unchanged)

Meta knows your ad is stale. Algorithm reduces delivery.

Action: Refresh creative or expand audience.

Signal #4: ROAS declines slowly (not sudden drop)

Gradual decline = audience exhaustion, not algo issue.

Action: Rotate to backup creative.

Signal #5: Negative comments/feedback increasing

"I've seen this 100 times" comments = creative is DEAD.

Action: Kill ad. Start fresh.

🔄 The Creative Refresh Strategy

Don't wait for fatigue. Refresh proactively.

The 3-Week Rotation:

  • Week 1: Launch Creative A (primary)
  • Week 2: Keep Creative A running. Prepare Creative B.
  • Week 3: If Frequency > 3.5, launch Creative B. Pause A.
  • Week 4: Repeat cycle.

Result: ROAS stays stable. No sudden drops.
