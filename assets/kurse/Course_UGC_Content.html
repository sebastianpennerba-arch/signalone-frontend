What is UGC (and Why It Outperforms Everything)

UGC = User Generated Content. Real people using your product. Not actors. Not studios. Just authentic testimonials.

UGC ads get 4x higher engagement than brand-produced ads. Here's why:

  • Looks organic (not like an ad)
  • High trust (real person, not brand)
  • Cheap to produce (€50-200/video vs €2000+ studio)

📝 The Perfect UGC Brief

Brief Template:

Product: [Name + Link]

Hook (First 3 sec): "I used to struggle with [problem]. Then I found [product]."

Body (Next 10 sec): Show product in use. Highlight 2-3 benefits.

CTA (Last 2 sec): "Click below to try it yourself."

Format: 9:16 vertical. 15 seconds max. Natural lighting.

Tone: Casual, authentic. Not salesy.
