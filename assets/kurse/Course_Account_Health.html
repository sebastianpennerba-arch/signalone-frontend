📊 Account Health Dashboard

What to Monitor Weekly (and Why)

⏱️ 45 Min
🟢 Beginner

Why You Need a Health Dashboard

Most marketers track 50+ metrics and ignore all of them. Instead, track 10 KPIs that actually matter. Check them weekly. That's it.

The Problem: "If I track everything, I manage nothing."

The Solution: Track ONLY the 10 metrics that tell you the truth.

The 3 Layers of Account Health

💰 SPEND LAYER

Are you spending the budget you planned?

⚙️ EFFICIENCY LAYER

Is it profitable? What's your ROAS?

📈 GROWTH LAYER

Are you growing month-over-month?

🔟 Trend

Week-over-Week change

Why These 10?

These 10 metrics cover all 3 layers of account health. Together, they tell you EVERYTHING:

  • If ROAS falls → Check CTR, CPA, Frequency (One of these 3 broke)
  • If Spend drops → Check CPM, Frequency (Algorithm is under-delivering)
  • If Profit falls → Check AOV, CPA, COGS (Margin problem, not traffic)

🎯 Set Your Thresholds (Green / Yellow / Red)

For each KPI, decide: What's OK? What's concerning? What's a crisis?

KPI 🟢 GREEN 🟡 YELLOW 🔴 RED
ROAS > 3.0x 2.0–2.9x < 2.0x
CTR > 1.5% 1.0–1.4% < 1.0%
CPA < Breakeven Breakeven ±10% > Breakeven +20%
Frequency < 3.0 3.0–4.0 > 4.0
Conversion Rate > 3% 2–3% < 2%

⚠️ Create a Threshold Sheet für dich selbst

Basierend auf deiner Industrie, Produkt-Typ und Gewinnspanne. Deine Schwellenwerte sind anders als meine.

🔄 The Weekly Health Check Ritual (20 Min)

Do this EVERY Monday morning. Takes 20 minutes.

Step 1: Pull Numbers (5 min)

Open Meta Ads Manager
Pull ROAS, CTR, CPA, CPM, Frequency for last 7 days
Compare to last week (7 days prior)

Step 2: Check Against Thresholds (3 min)

Is ROAS green / yellow / red?
Is CTR trending up or down?
Is Frequency above threshold?

Step 3: Identify Root Cause (7 min)

If yellow / red → Which metric broke?
CTR down? → Creative fatigue (change creative)
CPA up? → Targeting decay (expand audience)
ROAS stable? → All good, keep going

Step 4: Take Action (5 min)

Green across board? → Increase budget 20%
Yellow on anything? → Refresh that metric (new creative or audience)
Red on anything? → Diagnose immediately, don't wait

✅ That's it. 20 minutes, once a week. Your account stays healthy.

⚠️ Red Flags: When to Take Immediate Action

🚨 ROAS drops > 30% overnight

Likely: Creative fatigue, bid spike, or audience breakdown

Action: Pause campaign immediately, diagnose, then relaunch with new creative

🚨 Frequency > 5 + CTR drops 50%+

Your audience is tired. Ad has seen all the people it can reach.

Action: Pause, create new creative, relaunch in 3-5 days

🚨 Daily spend drops > 50% (unintentionally)

Meta's algorithm broke. Or your account got limited.

Action: Check billing, check account status, increase bid slightly

🚨 CPA > 2x breakeven

You're losing money. This won't fix itself.

Action: Pause immediately. Do CPA root cause analysis.
