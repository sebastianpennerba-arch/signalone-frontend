Why Retargeting is the Highest ROAS Channel

Fact: People who visited your site once are 10x more likely to buy than cold traffic.

But most people do retargeting wrong: "Show this ad to everyone who visited in last 30 days."

This course teaches you segmentation: different ads for different visitor behaviors.

The 5 Retargeting Segments

Segment Behavior Ad Type Avg ROAS
Viewed Product Clicked on product, didn't buy Show that exact product + discount 8-12x
Added to Cart Added but didn't checkout Urgency + free shipping 10-15x
Initiated Checkout Started checkout, didn't complete Strong urgency + guarantee 12-20x
Past Purchasers Bought once Upsell / Cross-sell 5-8x
Homepage Visitors Visited homepage only Awareness / Education 3-5x

Sequencing Strategy

Day 1: Show soft retargeting (testimonials, social proof)

Day 3: Show product benefits + urgency

Day 7: Show discount / last chance offer

This 3-step sequence converts 3x better than single retargeting ad.
