Why Most People Get Budget Allocation Wrong

Common mistake: "I'll split my €1000 budget evenly across 5 campaigns."

Result: Not enough budget for winners to scale. Too much budget wasted on losers.

This course teaches you the 70/20/10 rule used by agencies managing €1M+/month.

📊 The 70/20/10 Rule

70% → Proven Winners

Campaigns that already work. ROAS > 3x. Scale these.

Example: Your best performing campaign gets €700 of €1000 budget.

20% → Testing New Ideas

New hooks, audiences, creatives. Small budgets to find next winner.

Example: 4 test campaigns @ €50 each = €200 total.

10% → Retargeting

People who already visited. Cheap conversions.

Example: €100 retargeting budget.

Why this works: 70% on proven = stable revenue. 20% testing = future winners. 10% retargeting = easy wins.

🎯 Budget by Funnel Stage

Stage Budget % Goal
Awareness (Cold) 50% Get clicks, build audience
Consideration (Warm) 30% Nurture interest, educate
Conversion (Hot) 20% Close the sale

This is for NEW brands building an audience. If you have retargeting data, use 70/20/10 instead.
