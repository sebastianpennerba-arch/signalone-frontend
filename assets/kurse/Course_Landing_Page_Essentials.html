🎯 The 7 Essential Elements

Every landing page that converts has ALL 7. Missing one = conversion rate drops 30-50%.

1️⃣ HEADLINE (The Hook)

What the visitor sees first. Must match ad copy + promise outcome.

❌ "Welcome to Our Store" (Generic, no promise)

✅ "Get 50% More Leads in 30 Days (Guaranteed)" (Specific outcome + proof)

2️⃣ SUBHEADLINE (The Proof)

Backs up the headline with credibility. Social proof or authority.

❌ "Our product is great" (Vague claim)

✅ "Used by 10,000+ marketers. 4.8★ average rating." (Numbers + proof)

3️⃣ HERO IMAGE/VIDEO (The Visual)

Shows the product in use. Real people, real results. NOT stock photos.

❌ Generic stock photo of smiling people

✅ Screenshot of dashboard + real customer using it

4️⃣ CTA BUTTON (The Action)

One primary CTA. Clear, contrasting color. Action-oriented copy.

❌ "Submit" / "Click Here" (Passive)

✅ "Get My Free Template" / "Start 14-Day Trial" (Active + benefit)

5️⃣ BENEFITS (Not Features)

3-5 bullet points. Focus on outcomes, not specs.

❌ "Has 50GB storage" (Feature)

✅ "Never lose a file again – 50GB keeps everything safe" (Benefit)

6️⃣ SOCIAL PROOF (Trust)

Reviews, testimonials, logos, ratings. Must be real and specific.

❌ "People love us!"

✅ "4.9/5 from 2,347 reviews" + real customer quote + photo

7️⃣ URGENCY/SCARCITY (FOMO)

Real deadline or limited quantity. Forces decision.

❌ "Sale ends soon!" (Vague, unbelievable)

✅ "Only 3 spots left for Dec 2025 cohort" (Specific + real)

🔥 Above the Fold: The 3-Second Test

Above the fold = Everything visible without scrolling.

This is where 70% of visitors decide to stay or leave. You have 3 seconds.

The 3-Second Checklist

Can a visitor answer these 3 questions in 3 seconds?

1. What is this?

→ Headline must be crystal clear

2. What's in it for me?

→ Subheadline shows benefit

3. What do I do next?

→ CTA button is obvious

Above-the-Fold Formula

The Winning Layout:

TOP: Logo + Trust Badges (SSL, Reviews)
CENTER LEFT: Headline + Subheadline + CTA Button
CENTER RIGHT: Hero Image/Video
BOTTOM: Social Proof Bar (logos or review count)

This layout converts at 5-10%. It's proven.

📝 The Copy Formula That Converts

You don't need to be a copywriter. You need a formula.

PAS Framework (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

1. Problem: State the pain point

"Tired of low ROAS on your Meta ads?"

2. Agitate: Make it worse

"You're wasting €1000s on bad creatives. Every day."

3. Solution: Present your offer

"Our AI finds winning hooks in 24h. Guaranteed ROAS increase or money back."

BAB Framework (Before-After-Bridge)

1. Before: Their current state

"You're spending 10h/week on ad creative testing"

2. After: Desired state

"What if it took 10 minutes instead?"

3. Bridge: How to get there

"Our hook generator does it for you. Try it free."

🎨 Design Rules (No Designer Needed)

You don't need Figma skills. You need these 5 rules.

Rule #1: One CTA Color (Everywhere)

Pick ONE button color. Use it for ALL CTAs. Make it contrast with background.

✅ Example: Orange buttons on dark blue background

Rule #2: White Space = Good

Don't cram everything together. Space = clarity = higher conversion.

✅ Minimum 40px padding around sections

Rule #3: Max 2 Fonts

Headlines: Bold sans-serif. Body: Regular sans-serif. That's it.

✅ Example: Headline = Inter Bold, Body = Inter Regular

Rule #4: Mobile First

70% of traffic is mobile. Design for phone, then adapt to desktop.

✅ Test every page on your phone BEFORE publishing

Rule #5: Load Speed < 3 Seconds

Every second = 7% conversion loss. Compress images. No autoplay videos above fold.

✅ Test with PageSpeed Insights
